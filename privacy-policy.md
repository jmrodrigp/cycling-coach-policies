# Privacy Policy — Cycling Coach GPT

**Last updated:** July 2026

Cycling Coach GPT ("the App") is a personal-use Custom GPT built by Javier
as part of an individual AI-agent development project. This policy explains
how the App handles data accessed via the Whoop API.

## What data we access
The App requests the following data from Whoop, via OAuth 2.0, only for
the Whoop account holder who explicitly authorizes it:
- Recovery scores
- Sleep data
- Workout/activity data

## How the data is used
Data retrieved from Whoop is used solely to generate personalized cycling
training plan recommendations within the App. It is used only in the
context of the conversation in which it was requested.

## Data storage
The App does not persist or store Whoop data outside of the active
conversation session. No data is written to an external database, shared
with third parties, or used for any purpose beyond generating that
session's training recommendations.

## Data sharing
Data is never sold, shared, or disclosed to any third party.

## Revoking access
The Whoop account holder can revoke the App's access to their data at any
time via their Whoop account settings, or by contacting the developer
directly.
